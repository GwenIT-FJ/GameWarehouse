Slither.io

Control your snake, eat glowing orbs, and grow longer in this addictive multiplayer game. Challenge other players and become the longest snake in the arena!

Game Features

Fast-paced multiplayer action
Simple controls with arrow keys
Real-time multiplayer competition
Global leaderboard system

How to Play

1. Use arrow keys to control your snake's direction

2. Collect glowing orbs to grow longer

3. Avoid colliding with other snakes

4. Try to trap other snakes to make them crash

5. Become the longest snake to win!
