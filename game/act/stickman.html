Stickman Destruction

Stickman Destruction is an action-packed game where you control a stickman through various destruction challenges. Perform epic stunts, crash through obstacles, and cause as much destruction as possible to achieve high scores.

How to Play

  • • Use arrow keys to control movement
  • • Space to perform special actions
  • • Perform stunts to earn points
  • • Destroy obstacles for bonus points
  • • Complete challenges to unlock new levels
