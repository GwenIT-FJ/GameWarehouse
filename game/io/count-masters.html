Count Masters: Stickman Games

Count Masters is an exciting crowd runner game where you lead an army of stickmen through various challenges. Collect multipliers to grow your army and face off against enemy crowds in epic battles. Can you become the ultimate crowd master?

How to Play

  • • Drag left/right to move your crowd
  • • Collect multipliers to grow your army
  • • Battle enemy crowds
  • • Avoid obstacles
  • • Reach the finish line with the most stickmen
